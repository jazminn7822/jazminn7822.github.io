Freedom Project

The “Freedom Project” for SEP11 is all about creating a website using the knowledge we have gained throughout the school year on HTML and CSS. We will be informing high school students about a specific topic we decided to do research on. In addition, the purpose of the personal blog I have created, is to demonstrate the skills and knowledge I have gained along the way to build my website.

For my project, I will be making a website about thyroid dieseases, their sympotms and treatmets, using Github, HTML, CSS, and Jekyll. The blog will walk through my process of independently studying the thyroid gland and making a website about it using Jekyll.
